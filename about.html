Origin Story

In 1986, J. Duckets introduces California's first interracial Thai stand in a space barely 10 square feet on the corner of Baseline and Riverside Ave in Rialto, CA. Every day before dawn, J. visits the meat and produce markets to pick out fresh ingredients, which he prepared by hand. Meanwhile, his homie Moe took care of all the accounting for the new restaurant.
